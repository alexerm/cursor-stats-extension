Powerful Analytics for Your Coding Journey

Agent Messages Calendar

Visualize your daily AI chat requests with an interactive calendar heatmap. Track your engagement patterns and identify your most productive days.

Accepted Lines Tracking

Monitor accepted code suggestions over time. See how AI assistance contributes to your coding productivity and learn from usage patterns.

Token Usage Analytics

Track your token consumption with detailed bar charts for the last 30 days. Understand your usage patterns and optimize your AI interactions.

Live Data Fetching

Real-time data synchronization with progressive loading. Your dashboard updates automatically with the latest activity data.

Modern Dark UI

Clean, dark-themed dashboard designed for developers. Easy on the eyes with intuitive navigation and beautiful data visualization.

Privacy Focused

Your data stays private. The extension only accesses your own Cursor analytics and doesn't share any information with third parties.
